Gemini Watermark Cleaner
Smart Image Watermark Cleaner
Active
Version 1.0.1
An intelligent browser extension designed for Google Gemini that automatically detects and removes watermarks from downloaded images, providing a seamless high-quality image experience.
Key Features
  • Smart Detection - Automatically identifies watermarks in images
  • High Quality - Preserves image quality while removing watermarks
  • Fast Processing - Removes watermarks in milliseconds
  • Privacy First - All processing happens locally on your device
How to Use
  1. 1
    Visit gemini.google.com in your browser
  2. 2
    Browse and download images as you normally would
  3. 3
    Watermarks are automatically removed - enjoy clean images!
